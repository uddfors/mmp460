ABOUT ME

Charlotte Uddfors is a professional Designer with a concentration in the fields of Web Development, Digital Design and Branding. Introduced as “the best and brightest student” on AIGA NY website, Charlotte was nominated to present her Design Honors Project Research at the One in Ten: Gender Equality event in January 2018.

DEVELOPMENT

Digital Media and Ad Operations at Fast Company and Inc. Magazine. Designing, conceptualizing, programming, web animation, UI/UX, and front-end development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

DIGITAL DESIGN

Creative Director at SWEA NY. Leading and directing the creative process of all marketing collateral from concept to production.

BRANDING

College Collaborative Program with Goldman Sachs. Profound innovation in solving a series of business challenges related to financing, marketing, logistics and public relations.
