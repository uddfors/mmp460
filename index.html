ABOUT ME

Charlotte Uddfors is a professional Designer with a concentration in the fields of Digital Design, Web Development and Branding. Introduced as “the best and brightest student” on AIGA NY website, Charlotte was nominated to present her Design Honors Project Research at the One in Ten: Gender Equality event in January 2018. In addition, she was hand-selected to join the SWEA NY team as Creative Director and is currently leading and directing the creative process of all marketing collateral from concept to production. As of late December 2017, Charlotte have also completed an internship as Digital Media and Ad Operations Associate at Mansueto Ventures with a unique editorial focus on innovation in technology, leadership, and design for two of the world's leading and progressive business media brands, Fast Company and Inc. Magazine with an evolving role in Digital Advertising. The position requirements included designing, conceptualizing, programming, web animation, UI/UX, and front-end development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to improve the company's web presence.

Charlotte is currently pursuing a degree in Multimedia Programming and Design at The City University of New York and is a recipient of the Borough of Manhattan Community College Foundation Scholarship due to her outstanding academic accomplishments. As part of a College Collaborative Program, Charlotte has had the opportunity to work with Goldman Sachs. The program involves a profound innovation in solving a series of business challenges related to financing, marketing, logistics and public relations with supervision and guidance from Goldman Sachs mentors.
